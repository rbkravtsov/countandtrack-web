Full Policy

1. Information We Collect

Count & Track does not collect any personal information. The app does not require account registration, and no data is transmitted to external servers.

All data you enter — counter names, parameters, and event logs — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage.

2. Local Storage

Your data is saved on-device and is subject to standard iOS backup behavior. If you have iCloud Backup enabled, your app data may be included in your personal iCloud backup — which is controlled entirely by you through iOS Settings.

3. In-App Purchases

The lifetime unlock is processed through Apple's App Store infrastructure. We do not receive or store any payment information, billing details, or purchase history beyond what Apple provides through their StoreKit API (a transaction receipt used solely to verify your purchase status on-device).

4. Analytics & Crash Reporting

Count & Track does not use any third-party analytics or crash reporting services. If you have opted in to share diagnostics with app developers via iOS Settings → Privacy → Analytics & Improvements, Apple may share anonymized crash logs with us through App Store Connect. This is governed by Apple's privacy policy, not ours.

5. Third Parties

We do not share data with any third parties. No advertising networks, data brokers, or external services receive any information related to your use of this app.

6. Children's Privacy

Count & Track does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since no data is collected from any user, the app is suitable for all ages.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes materially, we will update the "Last updated" date below and, where appropriate, note the change in the App Store release notes.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at support@blooming-island.com.
