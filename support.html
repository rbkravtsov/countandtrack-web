FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I create a counter?


Tap the + button on the main screen. Give your counter a name, choose a mode, and add one or more parameters with colors. Each parameter is a type of event you'll be logging.

How do I log an event?


On the counter screen, tap the colored parameter button that matches the event. That's it — the event is logged instantly with a timestamp.

How do I undo the last event?


Shake your phone right after logging. A confirmation will appear to undo the last recorded event. This works for the most recent entry only.

What does "Stop Tracking" do?


It archives the counter — removing it from the main swipe view while keeping all your logged data intact. You can access archived counters any time from the full counter list.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?


Open the app, go to the paywall or settings, and tap Restore Purchase. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

Is there a subscription?


No. Count & Track uses a one-time lifetime purchase. Pay once and you own it forever — no recurring charges, no paywalled features later.

How long is the free trial?


7 days, with full access to all features. The app shows you how many trial days remain so there are no surprises.
